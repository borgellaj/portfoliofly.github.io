About Portfolio Fly Labs

Portfolio Fly Labs (PFL) is a Brooklyn-based MWBE fintech company that builds secure, auditable infrastructure to bring institutional-grade market analytics and trade-signal services to independent traders and small institutions.

What we do

PFL develops an AI-driven analytics platform and a modular signal-distribution engine. Our platform ingests market data, runs high-fidelity simulations, and produces transparent, timestamped signals for client-owned brokerage accounts. We emphasize security, regulatory-grade audit trails, and reproducible model governance.

Why it matters

Many advanced trading tools remain accessible only to large firms. PFL’s technology aims to narrow that gap by providing reproducible, auditable decision support while maintaining user control and custody of funds. We design systems for scale, observability, and compliance.

Operations & Technology (high level)

We use cloud-native compute, container orchestration, and managed data warehousing to run model training and backtests. Trade signals are distributed via secure APIs and integrate with off-the-shelf brokerage technology. For auditability, we maintain append-only records and cryptographic hashes of key events.
