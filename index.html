About Portfolio Fly Labs

Portfolio Fly Labs (PFL) is a Brooklyn-based MWBE fintech company building secure, auditable systems that empower individual traders and small institutions to manage markets with institutional precision. Our architecture fuses AI-driven analytics, market simulations, and compliance frameworks to create next-generation financial intelligence tools.

We focus on cloud-native infrastructure that lets clients retain full control of their accounts while benefiting from automated insights and signal orchestration. The platform is designed to scale globally and align with future regulatory standards for algorithmic trading transparency.
